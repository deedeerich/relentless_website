# Team Briefing Readiness Checklist

> Verify briefings are accurate, complete, and audience-appropriate before sending.

## Truth Labeling
- [ ] Data capture date stated
- [ ] Provider states listed (which scanners, which status)
- [ ] Known gaps explicitly called out
- [ ] Numbers carry denominators ("564 of ~800 estimated GHAS findings")
- [ ] Confidence level stated (authoritative / high / degraded / low)

## Audience Appropriateness
- [ ] Technical depth matches audience (leadership ≠ engineering)
- [ ] Decisions needed are clearly stated with options
- [ ] Recommendations have trade-offs, not just "do this"
- [ ] Urgency is evidence-based, not rhetorical

## Accuracy
- [ ] Numbers verified against source data (not memory)
- [ ] No conflation of accepted_risk with fixed
- [ ] No "100% coverage" without specifying denominator
- [ ] No "clean" without specifying scanner and scan date
- [ ] Cross-checked against LANDMINES.md for known traps

## Completeness
- [ ] All relevant decisions from DECISION_LEDGER.md referenced
- [ ] Active risks from risk register included
- [ ] Blocking items clearly identified
- [ ] Next steps are specific actions, not vague intentions
